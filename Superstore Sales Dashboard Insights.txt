Superstore Sales Dashboard Insights:

The average delivery time per order is 4 days.
Office supplies represent the largest share of sales.
In the sub-category, phones are the top-selling products.
Sales peaked in November and December for both 2019 and 2020.
Standard class shipping is the most preferred delivery method.
October was the most profitable month in both 2019 and 2020.
Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most commonly used payment method.
